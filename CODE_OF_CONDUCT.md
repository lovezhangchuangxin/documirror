# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

DocuMirror aims to provide a respectful, constructive, and technically rigorous
community space.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- being respectful in disagreements
- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent while discussing tradeoffs
- focusing criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
discussions, and other official communication channels.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to `2911331070@qq.com`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject
contributions, or take other moderation actions when behavior violates this
Code of Conduct.
